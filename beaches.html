Beaches in Sri Lanka

Beaches in Sri Lanka

Being surrounded by the Indian Ocean, There are beaches all around the country.

Nilaveli

A famous sandy beach with gentle waves because of the small reef around. And also the seafood specialized recipe’s in the little restaurants along the beach is popular with both locals and tourist.

Pasikuda

Pasikuda, meaning “green-algae-bay” is situated on the Eastern seaboard of Sri Lanka between Kalkuda and the Indian Ocean, approximately 35 kilometers from Batticaloa Town.
Other than the turquoise blue waters of the bay, local and foreign tourists are attracted to this wide sandy place under the hot tropical sun to surf, swim or just frolic in the water.

Unawatuna

Unawatuna beach is situated between five to six kilometers outside of Galle. It’s a crowded beach as many local and foreign visitors come here to enjoy the sight of the coral reef offshore.
